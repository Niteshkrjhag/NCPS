What These Scores Mean

Reliability (R*) — How often your votes align with the final credibility. Built from your voting history with time decay.

Experience (Exp) — Your accumulated activity level. More votes and posts → higher experience.

Trust (T) — Network-propagated trust score. Influenced by who you vote with and the trust of your peers.

Anomaly (Anom) — How unusual your behavior is. High anomaly means the system detects patterns similar to bots or manipulation.

Location Conf (L) — How confident the system is in your location. Based on GPS accuracy, movement plausibility, and consistency.

Weight (w) — Your final influence on post credibility. w = T × (1 − Anom) × Exp
